HERE'S HOW

Ever tried teaching someone how to complete a task on a website?
Ever spent hours following instructions on the web wishing there was a way to “show” you how it should be done? Look no further - Klickr.io offers an effortless way to record a website how-to that can be shared and replayed on any Chrome browser! We’re not talking video screencasts. We’re talking about a personalized in-situ playback.
